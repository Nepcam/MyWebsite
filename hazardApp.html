Health & Safety App

This was a project I started back in September 2018 when I did Enspiral Dev Academy. This project was for our final project of the Web Development bootcamp.

The application basically did all the Health and Safety Management systems automatically. For example, take in Hazard ID, Safety meetings, Near miss and accident register.

When our team presented this at the final night at Enspiral Dev Academy, I was approached by a Senior Developer to join them at Flux Federations Hack Day, where I got to experience this project in developement in a professional setting. I also got to experience being a product owner.

This application is currently on hold.

Technologies:

  • - Node JS
  • - React JS
  • - Redux JS
